About¶

pyxnat was originally developed at CEA/Neurospin and funded by the European project IMAGEN. The project was started in 2009 with Yannick Schwartz as the main developer but has since benefited from the help of a lot of people. We would like to give special thanks to the XNAT team at the Washington University of St Louis, and to the people at Neurospin.
